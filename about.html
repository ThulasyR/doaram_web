About DoAram

World's largest contribution platform

DoAram powers technology that transforms workplace culture to purpose driven business.

We, at DoAram, intuitively believe and aver that enhancing the quality of human lives is best achieved by integrating the people and endeavours with common objectives into a single unified platform with the help of state-of-the-art technology and processes. 'The people' here refers to all stake holders in the common cause including NGOs, Government Bodies, Corporate Houses, Business Enterprises, Social Entities and Individuals.

Our Vision

To build, sustain and provide the world's largest charity contribution platform for facilitating donations, skills, resources and planning with ease.

C

CORPORATES

Corporate Giants and Multinational Companies mostly have centralized their CSR decisions in their Headquarters though having offices and operations in multiple locations across the world.

This makes it difficult for them to select and run their own NGOs according to their Corporate philosophies and brand identities. Because of this poor selection process, the best performing NGOs are left unnoticed by these Corporate Giants.

N

NGOs

Though the NGOs have clear objectives and are steadfast towards their noble causes, most of them lag behind in marketing, technology and processes. Because of their shortfalls, they are not able to efficiently reach out to the stakeholders like Corporate donors, volunteers and resources to get funding, to follow transparent procedures and to implement effective mechanisms to fulfil their overall social objectives.
